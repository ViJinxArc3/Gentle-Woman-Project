How we started

The non-profit was founded in 2023 by a person who is really passionate about creating a platform for women to go for help. The platform was created to provide an escape portal. Think of the movie 'Narnia', four kids escaping a dislikable house through a mysterious closet and discovering a fantasy world. At first they were weak strangers and victems of the ice-queen but when they returned to reality, they returned as Kings and queens as they have fought their war to salvation. The Gentle Woman Project has the same motive. An Escape from a certain reality to come back as wholesome and greater than ever! Contact us if you know someone that needs to escaape.
